Company: TechCorp
Document: Privacy Policy and Data Security

---

Q: How is my data used?
A: Your data is used solely to provide and improve TechCorp's services. This includes processing your orders, personalizing your experience, sending service-related communications, and improving our platform based on usage patterns. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

---

Q: Do you sell my personal data?
A: No. TechCorp has never sold and will never sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (such as payment processors and delivery partners) but only to the extent necessary to provide our services, and under strict data protection agreements.

---

Q: What data do you collect?
A: We collect the following types of data:
- Account information: name, email address, phone number
- Payment information: billing address, last 4 digits of card (full card details are handled by Stripe and never stored by us)
- Usage data: pages visited, features used, session duration
- Device information: browser type, IP address, operating system

---

Q: How do I delete my data?
A: You can request deletion of all your personal data by going to Settings → Privacy → Request Data Deletion, or by emailing privacy@techcorp.com. We will delete your data within 30 days and send you a confirmation email. Note that some data may be retained for legal or accounting purposes as required by law.

---

Q: How do I download my data?
A: You can download a copy of all data we hold about you by going to Settings → Privacy → Download My Data. Your data will be prepared and emailed to you as a ZIP file within 48 hours.

---

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. We take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption and in transit using TLS 1.3. We are SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 compliant, and conduct regular third-party security audits.

---

Q: Are you GDPR compliant?
A: Yes. TechCorp is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union, and with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA). You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data at any time.

---

Q: Do you use cookies?
A: Yes. We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences, and analytics cookies to understand how our platform is used. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings or via our Cookie Settings page on the website.

---

Q: How long do you retain my data?
A: We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days. Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and tax compliance purposes.

---

Q: How do I report a privacy concern or data breach?
A: If you believe your data has been compromised or you have a privacy concern, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@techcorp.com immediately. We take all reports seriously and will respond within 72 hours. In the event of a confirmed data breach affecting your account, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by law.
