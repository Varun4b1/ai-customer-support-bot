Company: TechCorp Support
Document: Frequently Asked Questions

---

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a reset link within 2 minutes. The link expires in 30 minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact support@techcorp.com.

---

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel anytime from Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. We do not offer refunds for partial months. After cancellation, you can export your data within 30 days.

---

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. We do not accept cash, cheques, or bank transfers. All payments are processed securely via Stripe.

---

Q: How do I upgrade my plan?
A: Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately and you will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle.

---

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. We never sell your data to third parties.

---

Q: How do I contact support?
A: You can reach us at:
- Email: support@techcorp.com (response within 24 hours)
- Live Chat: Available Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm IST
- Phone: +91-22-1234-5678 (Business hours only)

---

Q: What is your refund policy?
A: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. To request a refund, email billing@techcorp.com within 14 days of your first payment with your order ID.

---

Q: How many users can I add to my account?
A: Starter plan: 1 user. Pro plan: up to 10 users. Enterprise plan: unlimited users. To add a user, go to Settings > Team > Invite Member.
